Odoo 17 Pharma Contact Approval (Enterprise)

How to use

  • State pending and approved added in customers.

  • Change in job position moved customer in pending state.

  • Pending customers will not be used in any partner field.

  • Approval button will be visible in customer for for selected supervisor.

  • By clicking approve button customer will be set to approved state.

  • After approve we can use it in the partner fields.
