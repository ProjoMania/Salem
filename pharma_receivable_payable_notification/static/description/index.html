Odoo 17 Pharma Receivable and Payable Notification (Enterprise)

This module sends notifications of pending payments.

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo Enterprise edition but not tested in Community version. OS.

Features

  • Sends a notification to the Salesperson regarding the customer's due amount.
  • Sends a notification to the Salesperson regarding the amount payable by the customer.

How to use

  • Create invoices and credit notes with desired due date.
  • If the invoiced amount remains unpaid for more than 30 days past the due date, a notification will be sent to the Salesperson of that invoice.
  • If the payable amount to customer remains unpaid for more than 30 days past the due date, a notification will be sent to the Salesperson of that credit note.
  • 'Due' button at the top is showing total due amount of partner.


  • After button click, it shows all invoices and credit notes records.



  • In Accounting > Customers > Invoices and Accounting > Customers > Credit Notes, under the 'Other Info' tab Salesperson is set.


  • Notifications for due payments are sent to the corresponding Salespersons after 30 days of due date. You can mark done, edit and cancel the activity.


  • Scheduled activity that sends notification.
